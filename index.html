About

Computer Science and Engineering is arguably the most sought after field in today’s continuously evolving world of innumerable and unprecedented technological advancements. What sets this field apart is its need for endless innovation and constant improvement; qualities that we, at the CSE Association of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, imbibe and embody. Ever since our inception, we have tirelessly strived to bring countless opportunities knocking on our students’ doors. From events highlighting the latest state of the art technology to competitions focussing upon not only technical but also non-technical aspects, we have done it all. Our goal is to grow with every passing day and provide a platform where students can enhance and hone their intellectual capabilities and skills. We aim to empower every student with the means to transform their inchoate ideas to groundbreaking wonders.
